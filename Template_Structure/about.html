WHO ARE WE?

Chefbound is a Vancouver-based company that promotes various chefs on a weekly basis based on food cultures. This platform allows people like you and me who are interested in trying specialty foods, celebrating a special night, or even hosting an event, to connect with extraordinary chefs. All ingredients used are guarenteed fresh for a healthy and delicious meal you won't forget. If needed, our service even offers food cooked right at your own home.
